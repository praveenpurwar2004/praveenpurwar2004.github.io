About the Visualization

This data visualization displays the Venture Market Fund analysis in different visuals using D3 concepts. Data used for the visualization is related to Venture Capital Funding over a time series Quarter by Quarter around the world.
Visualization represents the Venture Market Funding across all the regions of the globe(US & Canada) categorised in by Funding Status.

Recommended Settings

Most of the visualizations use the canvas of 1024x768 pixels and henceforth, the recommended viewing size of the interactive visualization is 1280x800 pixels.

Visualization

Three visualizations are used to represent the data.

Bubble Chart
Provides information about the complete dataset and also broken down by status

Line Chart
Line Chart provides information about the total investement by each status per Quarter/Year
Zoomable Sunburst Chart
Provides information in an aggregated format.Aggregation is done based on US and Canada Funding

Annotations

Annotations have been used in all three visualizations. For instance, in bubble and sunburst chart they are used to show per country Funding.

Parameters and Triggers

Parameters and Triggers are used across the visualizations.

Bubble Chart Visualization
In the bubble chart, three parameters are used

  • Overall Market Funding
  • Market Funding by Countries
  • Market Funding By Year/Quarter
On selection of the respective parameters by the user, a trigger will change the bubbleâ€™s x,y co-ordinates parameter to their respective groupings.

Zoomable Sunburst Visualization

Parameters and triggers are used based on the user mouse click actions. By clicking the Country, the trigger zooms into the data and provides underneath information.
By clicking the center, it will trigger the parameters to return to its values one up in the hierarchy.

Overview of All Funding Data

Bubble charts displays the Total Funding by Status , State , Country by respective markets for the period 2010-2014
